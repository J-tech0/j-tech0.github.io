Writer for Biotech and Innovation in Science


Location: Remote

Job Type: Unpaid Internship (Spring)

Category: Blog/Article Writing

Qualifications:
  • Students with majors in biology, biophysics, biochemistry, immunology, molecular biology, biophysics, medicine, or related areas.
  • Excellent grammar and writing skills.
  • Must be able to have fun!

Description:

Synergy Works Group – a startup IT and digital strategy consulting firm based in Bloomington, IN – is looking for students with a background in biology, biophysics, biochemistry, immunology, molecular biology, biophysics, medicine, or related areas.

Interns will assist in creating blog posts and whitepapers for a biotech company that is a client of Synergy Works Group. If you have a passion for science and are looking to gain a business perspective, this could be a great opportunity for you. You will get hands-on experience creating published content for a growing collaborative research organization and gain an understanding of how science can translate to a business environment.

If you are interested, please sign up using this form. From there, a member of our team will be in touch.

About Synergy Works Group:

Synergy Works Group is an IT and Digital Strategy consulting firm that provides multi-touchpoint guidance for small businesses and start-ups. At Synergy, we aim to create a custom solution for businesses using our diverse backgrounds and experiences. The company was created by current and former students of the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University.
