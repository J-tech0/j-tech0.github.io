Consultant Manager


Location: Remote

Job Type: Part-time, Paid

Category: Consulting


Description:

Synergy Works Group – a startup IT and digital strategy consulting firm based in Bloomington, IN – is looking for college students with a background in business and interest in consulting, management, and / or project management.

Consultant Managers plan, direct, and coordinate the operations of an assigned team of consultants. As a manager, you will enforce department policies, manage daily operations, and plan the distribution of client work to specific associates. You will get hands-on experience managing a team, working with clients, and developing yourself and teams’ personal and professional skills. Managers will occasionally consult with Directors and Partners for guidance and mandates. More details about the role responsibilities can be found in the sections below.

If you are interested, please sign up using this form. From there, a member of our team will be in touch.


About Synergy Works Group:

Synergy Works Group is an IT and Digital Strategy consulting firm that provides multi-touchpoint guidance for small businesses and start-ups. At Synergy, we aim to create a custom solution for businesses using our diverse backgrounds and experiences. The company was created by current and former students of the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University.


Qualifications:

  • Education:

    • Enrolled in 4-year college or university program in a related field(s)

  • Skills and Abilities:

    • Proficient in English written and verbal communication skills

    • Maintains a high degree of professionalism

    • Demonstrated time management and priority setting skills

    • Demonstrates a high commitment to quality

    • Possesses flexibility to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment

    • Seeks to acquire knowledge in their area of specialty

    • Highly thorough and dependable

    • Demonstrates a high level of accuracy, even under pressure

Fundamental Responsibilities:

  • Monitors consultant interns on their team and those interns’ projects to ensure that they efficiently and effectively provide needed goods or services within budgetary limits to assigned clients.

  • Reviews respective team’s financial statements, sales or activity reports, or other performance data to measure productivity or goal achievement or to identify areas needing cost reduction or program improvement.

  • Coordinates activities within the team concerned with the production, pricing, sales, or distribution of service

  • Directs administrative activities directly related to providing services.

  • Performs personnel functions such as training or evaluation.

  • Manages the movement of finished services to ensure quality, efficiency, effectiveness, and sustainability of operations.

Competencies:

  • Functional knowledge

    • Requires intermediate knowledge and understanding of principles and skills necessary to perform in the profession or particular area of specialization

    • Requires trained understanding of organizational technologies and practices

  • Problem solving and complexity

    • Requires the ability to identify and resolve moderately complex problems/issues/assignments

    • Requires trained problem-solving skills and techniques

  • Autonomy and decision making

    • Requires the ability to apply sound judgment and apply experienced analysis techniques within the area of specialization to make decisions

    • Decision-making authority is limited to the area of specialization

    • Sets priorities and/or sequence of tasks to achieve objectives with some oversight

  • Scope and impact

    • Duties and responsibilities have scope and impact that extend beyond the area of specialization

  • Leadership and influence

    • Influence is limited to the area of specialization and unit/team

    • Often provides guidance and support to junior-level peers
