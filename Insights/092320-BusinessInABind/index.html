Does 2020 have your business in a bind? Find new and exciting energy with Synergy!

September 23

Synergy Works Group was founded in the Spring of 2020 amid the novel coronavirus pandemic that shocked the world and displaced many students and graduates from their internships and jobs. Our mission was to help small businesses who were impacted the most by the pandemic while providing these students with an opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills.

Since then, we have been able to help over a dozen businesses start or grow their business with our services in Information Technology, Digital Marketing, and Growth strategies. We have worked with businesses in various industries such as photography, website development, and product retail and have seen each affected uniquely by the pandemic. In response to this, we created personalized solutions to meet the needs of their business.

As the year continues, it is increasingly clear that the novel coronavirus is not going away anytime soon. Along with this, we understand that small businesses are still facing challenges and struggling to overcome setbacks from the shutdown period. If this sounds like you and your business, we want to help you find new and exciting energy with Synergy Works Group!
