Internships

If you have been displaced from the Covid-19 crisis, we understand - and want to help! While we can't afford the same paid opportunities you had before, we are offering unpaid* internships to as many candidates as we can support. We want to help provide the essential experience, personal growth, and professional connections that are the basis for any business student as they approach the working world. Candidates who will succeed at Synergy have:

-Strong written/verbal communication skills

-Strong leadership skills

-Flexible and able to tackle tasks head on

-Strong analytical skills

-A desire to learn, improve, and succeed

-A team player

If you are someone who likes formulating multi-dimensional solutions to complex and unique problems for clients, Apply Now!
